# Revenue KPIs

## Total Revenue
Sum(payment_value)

## Average Order Value (AOV)
Revenue / Number of Orders

## Monthly Revenue
Revenue grouped by month

# Customer KPIs

## Active Customers
Distinct customers purchasing in a period

## Repeat Purchase Rate
Repeat Customers / Total Customers

# Product KPIs

## Units Sold
Count(order_items)

## Average Review Score
Average(review_score)

# Retention KPIs

## Churn Rate
Customers inactive for N days / Total Customers
